# 🔍 Loan Risk Analysis – Key Insights

This document summarizes the main findings from the loan risk analysis project.

---

## 📊 1. Employment Status vs Approval Rate

- Self-employed: ~27.8%
- Employed: ~24%
- Unemployed: ~18.2%

👉 **Insight:**  
Self-employed applicants have the highest approval rate, suggesting flexible or higher income sources improve approval chances.

---

## 📊 2. Loan Approval Distribution

- Approved: ~23.9%
- Rejected: ~76.1%

👉 **Insight:**  
The dataset shows a strong imbalance, indicating a conservative lending strategy.

---

## 📊 3. Education Level Impact

- Doctorate: ~44%
- Master: ~35%
- High School: ~14%

👉 **Insight:**  
Higher education correlates with higher approval probability.

---

## 📊 4. Income vs Risk Score

- Low income → Risk ~55  
- Very High income → Risk ~43  

👉 **Insight:**  
There is a clear inverse relationship between income and risk.

---

## 📊 5. Business Implications

- Income is the strongest predictor of risk
- Stable employment increases approval probability
- Lending strategy prioritizes minimizing risk

---

## 🚀 Key Takeaways

- Financial strength drives loan decisions
- Risk decreases as income increases
- Visualization improves interpretability
