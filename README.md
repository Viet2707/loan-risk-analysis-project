## 🔍 Key Insights

- Self-employed applicants have the highest approval rate (~27.8%)
- Loan approval rate is relatively low (~23.9%), indicating strict lending criteria
- Higher education levels correlate with higher approval probability
- Risk score decreases as income increases (inverse relationship)
- Income is the strongest factor affecting loan risk
